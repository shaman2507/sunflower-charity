Opening of the Children's Art Center "Sunflower"

Charitable Foundation "Fondacija Sunflower" (registration number PIB 11081932, Montenegro) starts the implementation of the charity program "Children and Art for Ukraine", which provides support in continuing art education and psychological rehabilitation of children and youth from different regions of Ukraine during the war. THE PROGRAM IS SUPPORTED BY THE EMBASSY OF UKRAINE IN MONTENEGRO. In the summer of 2023, the Charity Program "Children and Art for Ukraine" envisages opening of the Children's Art Center "Sunflower" (Budva, Montenegro). The program of the art center provides for 6 thematic shifts lasting 21 days each from 1.06 to 29.09.2023. Program of the art center foresees plein airs (open air art workshops) for young artists, work of summer vocal and choreographic schools and implementation of activities and excursion programs to meet the cognitive, educational and cultural needs of this charity program participants, their psychological rehabilitation and fulfillment of creative potential. According to the results of thematic shifts, charity exhibitions of paintings and performances by creative teams of "Kids and ART for UKRAINE" program participants will be organized. WE INVITE SPONSORS TO COOPERATE WITH THIS CHARITY ART PROGRAM TO SUPPORT UKRAINIAN CHILDREN. Charitable funds for the implementation of the Children's Art Center "Sunflower" work program will be used to pay for accommodation and meals for the participants of the charity program and organization of artistic and educational events and excursions. PARTNERS OF THE PROGRAM in Ukraine - NGO "International Educational and Cultural Association" International Educational Cultural Association and Charity Fund "International Education School".
